About the photographer

A Hanoian, who photographs Vietnam
with quiet devotion

My name is Trieu. I am a documentary and fine-art photographer based in Ha Noi.

"A photograph is not taken, it is given, by a moment that will never come again."

Since 2010, I have documented the lives of ethnic minority peoples, coastal fishing communities, and disappearing traditional crafts.

Tran Dinh Trieu, documentary photographer · Ha Noi
