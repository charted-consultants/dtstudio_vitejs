About AURA Studio

A studio that tells stories
through light

AURA was born from a simple belief: Vietnamese women deserve photos that celebrate their true beauty. No rigid posing, no heavy makeup, no retouching so heavy you become a stranger to yourself.

"I don't photograph beauty. I photograph the story of the person standing in front of my lens."

Since opening, AURA has walked alongside hundreds of clients in Nam Dinh. Every shoot is its own journey, every set of photos a chapter of memory no one else can have.

Duong Thi Hang, founding photographer of Blue Moon Studio

How It Works

From first contact
to receiving your photos

01
Day 1

I listen to you

Message me on Zalo or give me a call. I listen to your story and understand what you want before mentioning any package.

02
Day 1 to 3

I suggest a concept

I suggest concepts suited to your figure, personality, and story. We preview outfits, backgrounds, and the planned moodboard together.

03
Booking

Confirm and deposit

You lock in a date with a 30% deposit. Your session is confirmed and I begin preparing everything for the day.

04
Before shoot day

I send your prep list

2 to 3 days before, I send a detailed prep guide via Zalo — nails, hair, personal items, and small tips so you arrive feeling completely ready.

05
Shoot day

Makeup and wardrobe

Arrive at the studio and relax with my makeup artist. All outfits and accessories are already prepared. No rush, no pressure.

06
Shoot day

Unlimited shoot session

I guide you through posing, angles, and storytelling. I shoot until you love every single frame — no one will rush you.

07
Same day

Retouching and colour grading

I carefully retouch and colour-grade every photo in Blue Moon's signature style, enhancing your natural beauty without erasing what makes you uniquely you.

08
Within 24 hours

You receive your photos and keep them forever

All full-resolution originals delivered via Google Drive, kept forever, print-ready at any size, yours for any purpose.

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Hãy nhắn tin cho mình bất cứ lúc nào bạn sẵn sàng. Mình sẽ lắng nghe câu chuyện của bạn và từ từ tìm ra concept phù hợp nhất, không vội vàng, không áp lực.

What do I need to prepare before the session?

AURA prepares all outfits and accessories for the agreed concept. You just need to arrive on time with a relaxed mind. The team will send a detailed prep list via Zalo 2–3 days before, including tips on nails, hair, and things to note.

How long does a session take?

On average 2 to 4 hours depending on the number of concepts and your package. AURA sets no time limit. We shoot until you have all the frames you love. No one will rush you.

When will I receive my photos?

Fully retouched photos are delivered the same day or within 24 hours via Google Drive. Full-resolution originals, printable at A0 and above. You retain personal usage rights.

I don't know how to pose. Can I still shoot?

Absolutely. Our dedicated posing assistant guides you through every natural pose throughout the session. You don't need to know how to pose. Just relax and follow the guidance. The results will be more natural and beautiful than self-directed posing.

Can I book a group or couple session?

Absolutely. AURA welcomes couples, friends, families, and small groups. Contact us directly via Zalo for personalised package advice and an accurate quote based on group size.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations more than 72 hours in advance receive a 100% deposit refund. Within 48 hours: 50% refund. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable. Rescheduling is free if requested at least 48 hours in advance and subject to availability.
